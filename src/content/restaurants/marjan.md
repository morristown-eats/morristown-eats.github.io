Marjan is the kind of restaurant that locals who know about it treat like a secret, and everyone else hasn't found yet. Persian cuisine on Speedwell Avenue — BYOB, patio, a menu built around dishes that are specific enough to reward attention.

Strong vegetarian options throughout. A review base of over 330 Yelp reviews suggests the people who've found it come back. The kitchen has been doing this long enough that it shows.

The BYOB setup matters here: Persian food and a bottle of something good is one of Morristown's better combinations, if you know to look for it.
