Morristown Bagel and Deli has been open since roughly 1951. In a town where restaurants open and close with seasonal regularity, 75 years of operation is not a marketing claim — it's a fact that requires explanation.

The explanation is usually: the bagels are good, the operation is reliable, and the regulars are loyal. Family-owned throughout. Open from 6am, closed at 3pm, seven days a week. Morning only, and that's enough.

An Institution Award long-list candidate by timeline alone. Whether the food earns the distinction is a visit away.
