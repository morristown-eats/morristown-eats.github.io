---

Boston chain. Locations across New Jersey, DC, Maryland, New York.

Large, light room. First-come seating. Outdoor tables in summer. On weekdays it's mostly Sanofi workers from the building above. On weekends it fills with families — easier than somewhere that requires a reservation.

The pistachio latte is what people come for. We shared one on the way to Morris Proper next door.
